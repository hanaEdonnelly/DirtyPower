<p style="text-align: center; font-weight: bold; font-style: italic;">Modern Country Rock • Southern Roots • Electric Stage Presence</p>

Tanner Beane & Dirty Power are a Charleston-based band who aim to provide unforgettable high-energy entertainment full of crowd pleasers, throwbacks, and today's hits. Fronted by vocalist Tanner Beane, the band offers a tailorable setlist that spans generations and genres in order to give the audience the crowd-pleasers they know and love. The band also showcases original music at every show, giving audiences a taste of what makes Tanner Beane a rising country artist.

<hr>

Tanner Beane and Dirty Power prides themselves on proffesionalism and being easy to work with. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or venue booking, they bring the same level of professionalism to every stage.
